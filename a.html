🌿 Our Story

Who We Are

OrganoBite Foods is a farm-fresh organic food company dedicated to bringing nature's finest fruits and vegetables straight to your table. We started with a simple mission — to provide families with 100% natural, preservative-free produce that is both healthy and delicious.

Based in Pune, Maharashtra, we work directly with local farmers and trusted agricultural partners to source the best seasonal fruits. Our state-of-the-art IQF (Individually Quick Frozen) processing ensures that every product retains its natural nutrition, flavour, and freshness.

Our Mission & Vision

What drives us every single day.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide families across India with the freshest, most nutritious organic produce — harvested responsibly, processed hygienically, and delivered with care. We aim to make healthy eating accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone.

🌟

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted organic fruit brand, connecting farmers to families with transparency and integrity. We envision a future where every household has easy access to farm-fresh organic goodness — no compromises, no chemicals.
